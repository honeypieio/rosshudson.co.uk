Simple Strawberry Session Mead

My recipe for a strawberry hydromel (low alcohol mead). Ready to drink in 3-4 weeks. 2kg strawberries Frozen or fresh will do. Hull and then chop them into small (~2cm) chunks and place them into a pot.
